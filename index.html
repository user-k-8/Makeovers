About us

Welcome to Makeovers. We are a team of professionals who are passionate about connecting home builders with customers. Our mission is to make the home building process more accessible, transparent, and enjoyable for everyone involved. We understand that building or renovating a home is one of the biggest investments that people make in their lives, and we want to help them make informed decisions every step of the way. Our team consists of experienced professionals with a deep understanding of the home building industry. We work with builders who have a proven track record of delivering high-quality homes that meet their clients' needs and exceed their expectations. We take the time to get to know each client's unique needs and preferences so that we can match them with builders who are the best fit for their project. We also believe in transparency and honesty in our dealings. We provide our clients with all the information they need to make informed decisions about their home building project. Thank you for considering us for your home building needs. We look forward to working with you and helping you achieve your dream home.

Why Choose Us

Hassle Free

From connecting you with reputable builders to managing the paperwork, we take care of everything so that you can enjoy a seamless home building experience.

Well Constructed

We work with builders who use only the best materials and construction techniques to ensure that your home is well-constructed and meets the highest standards of quality and safety.

Free Utilites

Some our buliding partners offer free utilities, such as solar power, water recycling systems, and energy-efficient appliances, to help our clients save money and reduce their environmental impact..

Flexible Rentals

We offer flexible rental options that allow you to live in a dream home while we work on buliding yours. Whether you are looking for a temporary apartment or home, well help you find it.

Easy Financing

We work with a network of lenders who offer easy and flexible financing options to our clients. We take the time to understand your financial situation and match you with the lender who can offer the best terms for your needs.

Solid Paperwork

We take care of all the legal and administrative details, including permits, contracts, and other paperwork, to ensure that your project is legally sound and protected.
